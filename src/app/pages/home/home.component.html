The Monty Hall Problem

The Monty Hall is a brain teaser, in the form of a probability puzzle, loosely based on the American television game show Let's Make a Deal and named after its original host, Monty Hall.

Player is presented with three closed doors - behind one is a car, and behind the other 2 are goats. Player has to choose one of the three doors. while it remains closed presenter opens a door with a goat behind it. Now the Player is given an opportunity to switch to the remaining closed door or stick with the initial choice.

This simulation helps to undertand when the player chooses to switch the doors, there is a greater chance of wining the game than sticking into the first choice.
